Welcome to the College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS)

The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) at NISU is dedicated to shaping future leaders in the fields of Information Technology, Computer Science, and related disciplines. Through innovative teaching and hands-on learning, we prepare students to excel in the fast-evolving tech landscape.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Why Choose CICS?

CICS offers a supportive learning environment, state-of-the-art facilities, and a curriculum aligned with industry standards. Our graduates are equipped with the skills needed to thrive in tech-driven careers.
