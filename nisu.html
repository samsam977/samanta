About Northern Iloilo State University (NISU)

Northern Iloilo State University (NISU) is a prestigious educational institution located in Iloilo, Philippines. Renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, NISU offers a wide range of programs designed to prepare students for success in various fields. Whether you're interested in technology, business, education, or arts, NISU has something for you.

Programs Offered

  • Computer and Information Sciences (CICS)
  • Business Administration
  • Teacher Education
  • Engineering and Technology
  • Agricultural Sciences
